To use Cocktail Insights, will I have to install any tracking code on my website ?

No, Cocktail Insights does not require you to install any tracking code in order to use it.

Will Cocktail Insights present to me all my Google Analytics data ?

No, Cocktail Insights presents to you analytics and insights on post and campaigns that you publish from Cocktail Insights.

Will Cocktail Insights tell me the revenue generated by my social media post and campaigns ?

Yes, Cocktail Insights will let you know how much conversion and revenue that has been generated by a particular post or campaign.

Will I get historical data on post I created before using Cocktail Insights

Currently, Cocktail Insights does not provide historical data on post that were created outside the platform.

How much do I pay for using Cocktail Insights

You pay a percentage commission on your Ad spend which starts from 10% and decreases as your Ad spend increases. You also pay a monthly flat fee of $9 regardless of the amount you spend on your advertising campaigns.
